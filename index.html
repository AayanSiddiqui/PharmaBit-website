Our Vision

Accelerate the discovery of vaccine for new infectious threats
by leveraging Quantum Computing and Artifical Intelligence.

Step 1: Identify

Pharmabit identifies a virus through Polymerase Chain Reaction. This test enables us to identify the sequence of the virus through, amplifying and annealing (separate strands of) specific segments of the viral RNA/DNA.

Step 2: Immunological Memory

Our bodies are able to recognize certain pathogens that we have come into contact with previously. When the specific antigens are recognized, our body responds by triggering a corresponding immune response. Viruses trigger this immune response and Pharmabit is able to trace back to past vaccines and repurpose them based on the extracted RNA/DNA sequence.

Step 3: Simulation

Through simulation of the compound and immune cells, we can quickly discover how a potential candidate affects different populations over a period of years in a few minutes.

The Problem

The current drug discovery process takes multiple phases, spans several years, and costs several billion just to bring a new drug to market.

Pharmabit

A quantum simulation paired with smart AI algorithms allows for the possibility of speeding up the drug discovery process.

Solution

Pharmabit mass accelerates this process through quantum simulation and allows us to zero in on the perfect vaccine candidates using quantum annealing.

Impact

The world as a whole will be more prepared for any epidemics/pandemics in the years to come, and will help cure millions around the world.
